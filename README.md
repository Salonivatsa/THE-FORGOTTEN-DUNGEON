# THE FORGOTTEN DUNGEON

A Dark Fantasy Text Adventure Game.

## ⚔️ Overview
*The Forgotten Dungeon* is a browser-based, interactive text-adventure game where you play as a Seeker exploring the depths of an ancient dungeon, fighting monsters, unlocking secrets, and uncovering the lore of King Valdros and the dragon Aethon. 

The game features:
- **17 Unique Rooms** to explore
- **5 Chapters** of progression
- **30+ Actions & Commands**
- **10 Different Endings** depending on your choices and discoveries
- **Rich Inventory System** with weapons, armor, magical items, and lore artifacts

## 🎮 How to Play
Simply open `index.html` in your web browser. 

The game is controlled entirely via text commands. Type your actions into the input prompt at the bottom of the screen.

### Common Commands:
- `look` / `l` — Inspect your current surroundings.
- `go n/s/e/w` or `north/south/east/west` — Move between rooms.
- `pick up [item]` — Take an item you found.
- `inventory` / `inv` — Check your bag, HP, and status.
- `examine [thing]` — Look closer at an object in the room.
- `read [thing/book]` — Read an ancient text or note.
- `search` — Look for hidden items or secrets in the room.
- `use [item]` — Use an item from your inventory.
- `talk to [character]` — Talk to NPCs, ghosts, or creatures.
- `fight [enemy]` — Engage in combat.
- `status` — View your detailed stats and progress.
- `help` — See the full list of available commands.
- `restart` — Start a new journey.

## 📂 Project Structure
- `index.html`: The main entry point and structural layout of the game.
- `css/style.css`: Contains all styling, animations, and typography formatting.
- `js/script.js`: Contains the game engine, logic, commands parser, inventory management, room data, and event handling.

## 🛡️ Tips for Survival
- **Search Everywhere**: Always `search` a room before leaving; you might find hidden items or gold!
- **Read Your Journal**: Use the `journal` command to read lore entries. Understanding the dungeon's history is key to finding the best endings.
- **Prepare for Battle**: Don't fight the dragon without proper weapons, armor, or information about its weak point.
- **Pay Attention to Details**: Inscriptions on walls and notes left by previous seekers often contain crucial hints.
